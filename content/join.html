Join FOSS@KIIT

FOSS@KIIT is a volunteer driven society. Therefore anyone interested to contribute to the society in any form is welcome to join the society. Here are some areas where you can volunteer your time and effort, and contribute to the society:

  • Graphic design: Design graphics to promote the society and its events
  • Content authoring: Write content and blog posts for this website
  • Community engagement: Promote the society on Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, etc.
  • Development: Develop open source projects
  • System Administration: Maintain this website and server

If you want to contribute to this effort in one of these ways, or in any other way, or simply be a part of our community and hang out with us, shoot us an email at fosskiit@outlook.com or talk to us at #fosskiit.

Don't worry too much about whether you have the skills or time to make meaningful contributions to the community. We will help you get started. You are welcome to make as little or as much contributions to the society as time permits you. We will provide due credit to every contributor and volunteer on our website.
